4. Government's TATA MATS should not grant leave to the concerned engineers for any reason. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the MNS (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers / engineers transferred by the original office shall be entertained. If so received, it should be returned by post to the concerned officer / engineer at the original address. The application will not be entertained if the leave application of the transferred officer / engineer is forwarded to the parent office.
If the engineers in the above table do not appear at the place of immediate appointment in accordance with the said transfer order or attempt to cancel the transfer under any kind of pressure, the concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action on the ground that their action is in contravention of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services Rules (Conduct) 1979, treating it as misconduct.
The said Government Order is available on the website of the Government of Maharashtra www.maharashtra.gov.in.
