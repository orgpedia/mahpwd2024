Introduction: - The concept of Unique ID for each infrastructure project is to ensure proper planning of infrastructure works in the state, streamlining, balanced development of all areas and saving unnecessary costs. The Chief Minister is following suit. As per the Government decision of the Planning Department dated 9th June, 2025, a Unique ID has been implemented for every infrastructure project in the state.
Detailed instructions in this regard have already been issued through the relevant government circulars. Department of Planning. The following additional guidelines are being issued under the Guidelines dated 16th July 2026: -
Steps to be taken regarding Unique ID for infrastructure: -
1) It is mandatory to obtain an infrastructure ID (MHUID) prior to the administrative approval order of every public infrastructure and it shall be the responsibility of the administrative approving officer to ensure the same.
x) Instructions have been issued by the Planning Department under the relevant Government Decisions that it is mandatory to have an infrastructure ID while making infrastructure payments and that the payment should not be made unless the infrastructure ID is mentioned in the Administrative Sanction Order. In this regard, the Public Works Department, Govt. Detailed instructions were issued by the Government Circular dated 03rd December 2025. According to this detailed notice, for works in progress and materially completed but materially incomplete works, it will be given. Instructions were given to create a unique ID by 31 December 2025. Accordingly, before making payment for roads, bridges, building and other ongoing / in-progress works under the Department, the MHUID (Unique Infrastructure ID) of the work concerned should be verified by the concerned Executive Engineer before making payment.

3) In accordance with the additional guidance instructions of the Planning Department, it is mandatory to complete the registration of infrastructure completed / in progress / approved by the Administration from the date 01.04.2021 by conducting a special drive till the date 30.09.2026. Necessary action should be taken accordingly on priority.
4) In addition to the records mentioned in serial number 3 above, the infrastructure records dated before 01.04.2021 should also be taken on the Maharashtra Wealth Registration System (MASSAMPATHI) portal in a time-bound manner.
5) The competent authority concerned shall ensure that the above mentioned records are accurate and accurate.
6) All Superintending Engineers shall periodically review the action taken with respect to MHUID and submit a report to the Government.
