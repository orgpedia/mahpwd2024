Regulation of transfers of Maharashtra Government employees B. In accordance with the provisions of the Prohibition of Delays in the discharge of Government Duties Act, 2005 and with the approval of the competent authority, the engineers mentioned in Annexure-A attached to the Public Works Department shall be posted in front of their names in column No. 4 The position indicated herein is being transferred / posted.
It is communicated to the senior officers concerned that the engineer given the said appointment / posting should be immediately produced at the place of his / her new appointment. Also, the concerned engineers should immediately inform the government on the date they were relieved and appeared at the place of new appointment on the e-mail id doser2.pwd@maharashtra.gov.in.
However, officers whose services have been acquired for the Special In-depth Review Programme of Electoral Rolls-2026 out of the officers mentioned in the accompanying Annexure-A should not be relieved till the completion of this duty for joining the post of transfer / posting. Also, it should be ensured that the instructions of the Election Commission are not disregarded in any way.
(3) No leave shall be granted to the engineers concerned without the previous sanction of the Government for any reason whatsoever. Also, as per Rule-30 (c) of the MNS (Leave) Rules, 1981, no leave applications of officers / engineers transferred by the original office shall be entertained. If such applications are received, they should be returned by post to the concerned officer / engineer at the original address. Leave applications of officer / engineers transferred to the parent office will not be entertained.
Since the officers mentioned in Annexure-A are being transferred on their request, they will not be paid the period of assumption of office, migration allowance and daily allowance.
If the engineers mentioned in the accompanying Annexure-A do not appear at the place of immediate appointment in accordance with the said transfer order or attempt to cancel the transfer under any kind of pressure, the concerned shall be liable to disciplinary action on the ground that their action is in contravention of Rule 23 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, treating it as misconduct.
6. The officers mentioned in Annexure-A to be transferred on their request shall not be liable to pay the period of assumption of office, emigration allowance and daily allowance.
